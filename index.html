Fried Rice

Makes about two to three hearty servings.

main ingredients:

  • 1 x cup of rice
  • ½ x onion
  • 2 x medium carrots
  • 4 x green onions
  • 6 x eggs
  • 1 lb. of protein (beef, chicken, spam, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp. sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce

seasoning:

  • sesame seeds
  • salt

Old Fashioned (cocktail)

Makes one drink.

main ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Bourbon/Rye Whiskey
  • ¼ oz. Rich Brown Syrup
  • 3-6 dash Angostura Bitters
  • Orange peel, oils expressed and garnish

Rich Brown Syrup

30 days shelf life, refrigerated

  • 2:1 ratio of brown sugar to water, ideally by weight w/ scale

25ml yield (for casual/occasional drinking)

  • 40 g Turbinado Sugar
  • 20 g Filtered Water

Lemonade

Makes about 5 small mason jars.

main ingredients:

  • 1 x cup sugar
  • 1 x cup lemon juice (about 7 lemons)
  • 7 x cups water

Pan-grilled Steak

Makes about 2 hearty servings.

main ingredients:

  • 1 lb. NY prime steak
  • 1 lb. prime rib

seasoning:

  • salt
  • pepper
  • rosemary
  • oil
  • butter

Instructions

2 minutes each side on med-high heat. Finish 4 minutes in 360° oven.
