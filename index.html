Fried Rice

Makes about two to three hearty servings.

main ingredients:

  • 1 x cup of rice
  • ½ x onion
  • 2 x medium carrots
  • 4 x green onions
  • 6 x eggs
  • 1 x pound of protein (beef, chicken, spam, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp. sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce

seasoning:

  • sesame seeds
  • salt

Old Fashioned (cocktail)

Makes one drink.

main ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Bourbon/Rye Whiskey
  • ¼ oz. Rich Brown Syrup
  • 3-6 dash Angostura Bitters
  • Orange peel, oils expressed and garnish

Rich Brown Syrup

30 days shelf life, refrigerated

  • 2:1 ratio of brown sugar to water, ideally by weight w/ scale

25ml yield (for casual/occasional drinking)

  • 40 g Turbinado Sugar
  • 20 g Filtered Water

Spaghetti Meat Sauce

Makes about four hearty servings.

main ingredients:

  • 1 x spaghetti noodles
  • 4 x tomatoes
  • 1 x whole onion
  • 1 x whole garlic
  • 1 x can tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • mushrooms (optional)
  • carrots (optional)
  • basil leaves (optional)

seasoning:

  • salt
  • chili flakes
  • oregano
