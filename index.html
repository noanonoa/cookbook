Spaghetti Meat Sauce

Makes about four hearty servings.

main ingredients:

  • 1 x spaghetti noodles
  • 4 x tomatoes
  • 1 x whole onion
  • 1 x whole garlic
  • 1 x can tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • mushrooms (optional)
  • carrots (optional)
  • basil leaves (optional)

seasoning:

  • salt
  • chili flakes
  • oregano
